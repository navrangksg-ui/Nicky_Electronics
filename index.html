Our Story

38 Years of Trust in Every Home

❧

Nicky Electronics is not merely a store — it is a living legacy woven into the fabric of Kishangarh. Since 1986, we have stood as the most trusted name in home appliances and electronics across Rajasthan, built not through advertising, but through honest relationships and unwavering integrity.

For nearly four decades, thousands of families have walked through our doors — some as newlyweds furnishing their first home, some as parents seeking the perfect television for their children, some returning for the third generation. Each one has been welcomed not as a customer, but as family.

From the cool relief of an air conditioner on a scorching Rajasthani summer to the warmth of a television screen on winter evenings — every product we offer has been chosen to bring comfort, joy, and lasting value to your home.
